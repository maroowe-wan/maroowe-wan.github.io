Now, why is Kafka so fast? Well, the&nbsp;

A Kafka topic is a higher-level abstraction&nbsp;
that consists of partitions. A partition&nbsp;&nbsp;

Now let’s talk about Producers. Producers are&nbsp;&nbsp;

Consumers are applications that&nbsp;

Let's take a look at what a Kafka cluster looks&nbsp;
